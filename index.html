Printing Guide

Material: Why PETG?
For heavy sustained loads (rock slabs/spheres), PETG is ideal. Unlike PLA, it doesn't "creep" (deform over time) and is less brittle. It handles impact better and is easy to print.

Format: Why 3MF?
3MF is the modern standard. Unlike legacy STL, 3MF contains strict unit definitions and clean XML structure, preventing mesh errors.
